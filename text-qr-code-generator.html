When to Use a Plain Text QR Code

Use plain text QR when the information does not fit any specific protocol (URL, tel:, mailto:, etc.). Common uses include product codes, discount codes, event tokens, short instructions, and any custom message you want to distribute in scannable form.

Character Limit and Complexity

The more characters you encode, the denser the QR pattern becomes. Dense QR codes need better lighting and camera resolution to scan reliably. Keep text under 300 characters for wide compatibility, especially if the QR will be printed small.
