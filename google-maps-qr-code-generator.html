Google Maps QR for Businesses

A Google Maps QR code opens navigation to your location when scanned. Customers never have to manually search for your shop — they just scan and Google Maps shows them the route from wherever they are.

Best Uses

  • Business cards and pamphlets
  • Packaging and delivery inserts
  • Website "Find Us" section — for mobile users
  • Event invitations
  • WhatsApp messages with your stall or venue

Tips

Use the exact address from Google Maps for precision. For popular landmarks, the place name alone usually works well. Always test the QR on your phone before printing.
