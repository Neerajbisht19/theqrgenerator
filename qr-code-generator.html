What is a QR Code Generator?

A QR code (Quick Response code) generator is a tool that converts information — a website URL, phone number, WiFi password, payment ID, or any other data — into a square barcode that any smartphone camera can read instantly.

The QR Generator creates high-quality QR codes in your browser, so your data never leaves your device. It's free, instant, and requires no account or credit card.

How to Use This Generator

  1. Choose a type — website URL, WhatsApp, UPI, WiFi, or any of the 12 available types.
  2. Enter your details — fill in the fields relevant to your chosen QR type.
  3. Customize — pick a style (classic, dots, or gradient), choose a color, and select the output size.
  4. Download — save as PNG for digital use or SVG for print (infinitely scalable).

Which QR Type Should I Use?

Use Website URL for any webpage. Use WhatsApp to let people chat with you without saving your number. Use UPI Payment to accept money at your shop. Use WiFi so guests can connect automatically. Use Google Maps so customers can find your location.
