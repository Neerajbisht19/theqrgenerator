Why We Built This

QR codes have become part of daily life in India — on shop counters for UPI payments, on menus for digital ordering, on packaging to build social followings. But most QR generators are built for Western markets, loaded with upsells, and require creating accounts just to download a simple code.

The QR Generator was built from the ground up for Indian users and businesses — with UPI as a first-class citizen, WhatsApp deep links that work correctly, and a clean, fast experience that respects your time.

Our Philosophy

  • No watermarks. Ever. Commercial use, personal use — your QR code is yours.
  • No accounts. No sign-up friction. Open the page, generate, download, done.
  • Privacy first. All QR generation happens in your browser. Nothing is sent to our servers.
  • Fast. No server round-trips. QR codes generate in milliseconds.

What We Support

We support 12+ QR types: Website URL, WhatsApp, UPI Payment, WiFi, Instagram, YouTube, Google Maps, Email, SMS, Phone, Twitter/X, and Plain Text. Each type has its own dedicated page with purpose-built fields and format handling.
