Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how The QR Generator ("we", "us", "our") at theqrgenerator.in handles information when you use our service.

The Short Version

We collect almost nothing. QR codes are generated entirely in your browser. Your data never touches our servers. We use basic analytics to understand traffic. That's it.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • QR code content — generated entirely client-side (in your browser). We never see what you encode.
  • Names, email addresses, or personal identifiers — no account required.
  • Payment information — we charge nothing, collect nothing.

Information We May Collect

Analytics

We use privacy-respecting analytics (Google Analytics with IP anonymisation enabled) to understand which pages are popular, what devices visitors use, and which countries our users are in. This is aggregate data — we cannot identify you individually.

Advertising

We display Google AdSense advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalise ads. You can opt out via Google's Ad Settings. We do not control the data Google collects for advertising purposes.

Server Logs

Our hosting provider may collect standard server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages visited. These are used for security and performance purposes and are retained for a maximum of 30 days.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies. Google Analytics and Google AdSense set their own cookies. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • Google Fonts — typography (fonts loaded from Google servers)
  • Cloudflare CDN — QRCode.js library

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
